Welcome to The Hideaway

The Hideaway sits in a snug nook on Main Street in Minneapolis, looking out on the Mighty Mississippi. A true four-season gem, The Hideaway has one of the greatest decks in the city, and a classic north-woods interior. Great burgers, cold beer, affordable cocktails, music, and a couple of big TVs (only on during big games) make The Hideaway a comfortable and relaxing hang.
